Waterfall
- Pros: Predictable timeline; strong documentation; compliance-friendly
- Cons: Rigid; late UX discovery; costly changes
- Fit: Low-uncertainty, fixed-scope

Agile/Scrum
- Pros: Iterative delivery; fast feedback; prioritization; visibility
- Cons: Needs strong product ownership; scope creep risk
- Fit: Evolving requirements, user-facing products

Kanban
- Pros: Continuous flow; WIP limits; simple adoption
- Cons: Weaker timeboxing; goal drift risk
- Fit: Ops/maintenance; continuous improvements

Spiral
- Pros: Risk-driven; early mitigation
- Cons: Complex management; cost overhead
- Fit: High-risk/complex systems

V-Model
- Pros: Strong test alignment; clear verification/validation
- Cons: Rigid; heavy upfront design
- Fit: Safety-critical
