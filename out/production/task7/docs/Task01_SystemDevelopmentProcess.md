1. Planning and Gathering Requirements
- Stakeholder interviews, market review, scope, budget/timeline, tool selection.
- Purpose: Feasibility and user-centered goals (mobile-friendly, secure login, result sharing).

2. Defining Requirements
- SRS drafted and approved by stakeholders.
- Purpose: Clear roadmap and prevent ambiguity.

3. Designing the System
- DDS with UX flows, architecture, security/privacy plan.
- Purpose: Usable, scalable, secure structure.

4. Building the System
- Implement Java/Spring Boot services, UI, persistence, authz/authn, notifications.
- Purpose: Quiz logic, scoring, anti-cheat randomization.

5. Testing the System
- Unit, integration, E2E/UI tests; performance; cross-browser; help files.
- Purpose: Correctness, reliability, accessibility.

6. Launching and Maintaining
- Staged rollout, monitoring, patches, new features.
- Purpose: Reliable operation and continuous improvement.
