Requirement Types
- Functional: login, catalog, randomization, scoring, review, sharing, admin question management, attempt history.
- Non-functional: performance, security, reliability, usability, portability, maintainability.
- Constraints: GDPR, data residency, budget/tooling, browser support.
- Business rules: pass threshold, one attempt policy, role-based access.

Elicitation Techniques
- Interviews (stakeholders), surveys (target users), workshops (joint modeling), observation (contextual inquiry), prototyping (wireframes/mockups), document analysis (existing materials), analytics review.

Standards
- SRS: IEEE 830 / ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148.
- Modeling: UML (Use Case, Sequence, Activity, Class); BPMN when needed.
- Security/Privacy: OWASP ASVS; GDPR compliance (data subject rights, consent, retention).
